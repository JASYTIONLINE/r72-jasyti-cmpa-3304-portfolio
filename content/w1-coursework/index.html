Week 01 Exercise 02: Field Trip to the Web Design Museum

For my web site to analyze I chose a 1996 Pepsi a site that looks exciting but struggles with mapping and affordability. The layout is visually dense, which makes it hard to understand what the site is trying to accomplish or what I should do once I arrive. The "Enter" button is present but not mapped in a way that draws the user’s attention, and the overall design feels more like a TV promotion than a product site. The result is an experience that looks fun but lacks clarity and purpose.
