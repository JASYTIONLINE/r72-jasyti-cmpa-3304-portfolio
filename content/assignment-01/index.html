Top 5 Songs I've Written

  1. We Are the Guard

    “We Are the Guard” is a patriotic song that tells the story of the National Guard — who we are and what we stand for. For more than 300 years, the Guard has been America’s first military force, born from the early citizen militias that protected their colonies long before our nation was formed. This song captures that spirit — duty, pride, and the enduring truth that the National Guard is always there when America calls.

  2. A Shadow Falls

    “A Shadow Falls” tells the story of a wounded soldier lying on the battlefield in his final moments, reflecting on his life and regrets. It’s a tribute to those whose lives ended too soon, before they had the chance to make the mark they dreamed of.

  3. Transformers: Not the Autobots You're Looking For

    “Transformers: Not the Autobots You’re Looking For” is a fun educational song that uses pop culture to explain artificial intelligence. It draws a clever parallel between classic Transformers and modern AI models like GPT, making the topic engaging and easy to understand.

  4. Drivin’ Way Too Fast

    This song tells the story of my youthful years — chasing adventure with my first car and later transitioning into adulthood. It's a nostalgic ride through memory lane and the lessons I learned along the way.

  5. I Once Crawled Home: Tip the Glass

    A lighthearted, honest anthem about the unforgettable nights out with friends. It's a toast to bad decisions, good times, and the hilarious stories that come out of them.
